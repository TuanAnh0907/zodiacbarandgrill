Welcome to Our Restaurant

Zodiac Bar and Grill has been a downtown Jacksonville staple for more than 19 years. It has transformed from a local lunch venue into a full service catering and nightlife destination. The Chef blends a fusion of authentic Mediterranean cuisine with American flavors and foods. Join us for lunch to enjoy our popluar buffet.

Private Events & Catering

Make us your home for special events! Zodiac Bar & Grill offers private dining for birthday parties, corporate events, receptions and more. With a dedicated service team and custom menu packages, our inviting atmosphere and authentic Mediterranean cuisine is sure to make any event a special one.

We also offer catering services—let us bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your next home, office or private event.

Give us a call or stop in today to schedule your next dining experience!
